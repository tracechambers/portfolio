The Beforeland

For the logo design of this modern dinosaur-themed hotel, I aimed to blend prehistoric essence with contemporary elegance. The logo features a stylized dinosaur silhouette, balancing strength and sophistication. Earthy tones evoke the natural environment, while vibrant accents add modernity and excitement.

Clean, bold lines ensure recognizability across various mediums, and the modern sans-serif typography complements the silhouette. Subtle textures add depth, hinting at rugged terrains while maintaining a polished look. This fusion creates a unique visual identity, promising a distinctive and memorable experience.
